About

Theory meets experiment

EULER — Grupo de Investigación Energía y Materiales — conducts theoretical and experimental research on new materials for energy conversion and storage. We integrate chemical synthesis, advanced characterization, and high-performance DFT modeling to predict physical properties and optimize energy devices. Founded 2024 at UNJBG, funded by Canon Minero.

Develop nanomaterials for energy conversion and storage.

Synthesize and characterize compounds with advanced spectroscopic methods.

Run HPC simulations for electronic and transport properties from first principles.

Optimize thermoelectric ZT and ionic conductivity computationally.
